NORMAL WOOD SUPPLY

Premium quality raw wood for furniture, construction, and interior design

Furniture

Premium wood for tables, chairs, beds, and wardrobes. We offer Sheesham, teak, and mango wood, popular choices known for their stunning aesthetics and natural beauty.

Construction

High-quality wooden beams, columns, and flooring for traditional architecture. We provide Sal and teak wood, renowned for their exceptional strength and durability.

Interior Design

Elegant wood solutions for paneling, flooring, and decorative elements. Our hardwoods like teak and oak are perfect for creating sophisticated interior spaces.

TREATED WOOD SUPPLY

Chemically modified wood for enhanced durability and specific applications

Outdoor Applications

Weather-resistant treated wood perfect for decking, fencing, pergolas, and gazebos. Our treated wood ensures long-lasting durability in outdoor environments.

Marine Applications

Specially treated wood for docks, piers, and boat construction. Enhanced with protection against water damage and decay for maritime environments.

Industrial Use

Pressure-treated wood for structural elements, scaffolding, and industrial applications. Guaranteed protection against infestation during transportation and use.
